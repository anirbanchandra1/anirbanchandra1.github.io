**Focus:** Enhancing the performance and reliability of next-generation hardware through electro-thermal computational modeling.
* **Phase Change Memory (PCM):** Optimized reset characteristics and resistance drift in PCM cells using heater configuration analysis and porous dielectric insulation.
* **GaN Thermal Management:** Investigated growth temperatures and interfacial thermal conductance in **GaN/AlN heterostructures** to mitigate heat dissipation issues in high-power chips.
