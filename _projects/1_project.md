**Focus:** Accelerating computationally expensive simulations using neural operators and graph-based architectures.
* **Carbon Capture & Storage (CCS):** Developed surrogate modeling frameworks using **Fourier Neural Operators (FNO)** to simulate $CO_2$ plume migration and pressure evolution in realistic geologies. This enables real-time site screening and uncertainty quantification.
