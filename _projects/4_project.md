**Focus:** Investigating atomistic and continuum physics of heat/mass transfer at liquid-vapor interfaces + Engineering robust numerical frameworks for multiphase  flows.
* **Interfacial Dynamics:** Conducted **Molecular Dynamics (MD)** and continuum studies on transient evaporation/condensation. Research focused on temperature discontinuities at interfaces and the validity of Schrage relationships for water.
* **Interface Tracking:** Developed parallel computational infrastructures and **stabilized finite element methods** for explicit phase tracking in compressible multiphase processes.
