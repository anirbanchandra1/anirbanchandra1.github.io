**Focus:** Integrating AI with physical manufacturing to enable "Digital Twins" and material design.
* **Interatomic Potentials:** Created **Reinforcement Learning-based** hybrid bond-order coarse-grained potentials to explore mesoscale molecular aggregation in liquid-liquid mixtures.
* **Additive Manufacturing (AM):** Developed frameworks for featurizing microstructural images to identify structure-processing relationships in metal 3D printing, focusing on dimensional control and uncertainty quantification.
